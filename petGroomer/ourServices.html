Our Services

We offer the very best in customer service and our highly qualified and caring staff is dedicated to providing the very best in pet grooming. We understand the needs of your pet and we strive to fulfill them.

Itchy? Dry? Flaky? Smelly?

You've come to the right place. We do everything from conditioning the coat to reduce matting and shedding to treating dogs with serious skin problems. The method is different for each dog. We use a combination of products that deep clean, disinfect, sooth, and re-moisturize the skin and coat.

We can take care of the flakes, grime, and odor.

  • Treatments available at extra charge.
  • Due to the skin condition of some breeds, some treatment may be recommended to prevent skin irritation or ensure a thorough cleaning.

*** Bath & Tidy includes...

The Bath & Tidy Package includes shampoo, massage and blow dry, hand brushing and loose hair removal, nail trimming, ear cleaning, and bow or bandanna. Cost varies by size and type of breed.

*** Full Groom includes...

The Full Groom includes all services from a Bath & Tidy, as well as haircut. Cost varies by size and type of breed.

*** Nails & Ear Care includes...

Nails & Ear Care includes nail trimming, ear cleaning, breath freshening.

***Puppy Brush & Trim includes...

Puppy Brush & Trim includes shampoo, massage and blow dry, brushing, face, feet and tail trim; nail trimming, ear cleaning, and bow or bandanna.
